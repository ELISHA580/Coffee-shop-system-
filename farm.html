Coffe arabica

Also know as the Arabian coffe ,is a species of flowerng plant in the coffee and madder family Rubiacease. it is believed to be the first specie of coffee to have been cultivated ,and is currently the dominant cultivar representing about 60% of global production
Arabica coffee originates from Ethiopia and was cultivated i Yemen and documented by the 12th century .

The following are the requirements to better cultivation
  • Arabica takes approximately seven years to mature fully
  • And does the best with 1.0-1.5 metres(39-59 in) of rain
  • Usually cultivated at the altitude between 1300 and 1500 m
  • The plant can tolerate low temperatures, not frost
  • And does it best in temperature between 15 and 240C
